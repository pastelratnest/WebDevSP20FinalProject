ABOUT

Matt Clepper is a photographer and visual artist (only, for the sake of making this project brief!) born in Dallas and based in Chicago. Working in both analog and digital formats, they seek to bridge the gap between phenomena and feeling by framing the world and its happenings in a distinctly personal context.
